# Bike Sales Excel Dashboard

## Project Overview
The **Bike Sales Excel Dashboard** is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze customer demographics, purchasing behavior, and factors influencing bike purchases. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.

## Purpose
This dashboard enables users to analyze bike sales data, identify purchasing trends, compare customer demographics, and understand how factors such as income, age, gender, education, occupation, region, and commute distance influence bike purchases.

## Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
- 📊 Microsoft Excel – Used for data analysis and dashboard creation.
- 📂 Data Cleaning – Cleaned and prepared raw data for analysis.
- 📈 Pivot Tables – Summarized and analyzed large datasets.
- 📉 Pivot Charts – Created dynamic visualizations.
- 🎛️ Slicers – Enabled interactive filtering across dashboard visuals.
- 📁 File Formats – `.xlsx` for development and `.png` for dashboard preview.

# Features & Highlights
## Business Problem
Businesses often collect large amounts of customer data but struggle to identify which customer characteristics influence purchasing decisions.
Key questions include:
- Which age group purchases bikes the most?
- Does income affect bike purchases?
- How does commute distance influence buying behavior?
- Which regions generate the highest bike sales?
- Are there noticeable differences between male and female customers?
## Goal of the Dashboard
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify factors affecting bike sales.
- Compare sales across different customer demographics.
- Provide interactive business insights.
- Support data-driven decision making.
## Key KPIs
- Total Customers
- Total Bike Purchases
- Average Customer Income
- Purchase Rate
- Average Age of Customers

## Dashboard Highlights

### Average Income per Purchase

Compares the average income of customers who purchased bikes versus those who did not.

### Customer Age Analysis

Displays bike purchases across different age groups such as Adolescents, Middle Age, and Old.

### Customer Commute Distance

Analyzes purchasing behavior based on commute distance.

### Regional Sales Analysis

Compares bike purchases across different geographical regions.

### Gender Comparison

Shows purchasing patterns between male and female customers.

### Interactive Filters

Allows users to filter the dashboard by:

- Marital Status
- Education
- Region
- Occupation

---

## Business Insights

- Customers with higher average income were more likely to purchase bikes.
- Middle-aged customers represented the largest group of bike buyers.
- Shorter commute distances showed higher purchase rates.
- Regional differences influenced overall bike sales.
- Interactive filters allow deeper analysis of customer purchasing behavior.
